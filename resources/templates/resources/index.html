Immediate Coping Strategies

Deep Breathing (4-7-8)

Inhale loudly for 4 seconds, retain breath logically for 7 seconds, and heavily exhale completely for 8 seconds. Repeat 4 times heavily reducing cortisol vectors.

Grounding (5-4-3-2-1)

Focus explicitly finding 5 things you can visually see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear, 2 you smell, and 1 you specifically taste.

Progressive Relaxation

Slowly tense and relax individual muscle groups aggressively starting directly from your feet incrementally upward directly relieving somatic stress systematically.
