What is MindMappR?

MindMappR is a mental wellness tracking platform designed to help you understand your mood patterns, take structured depression assessments, track emotional progress over time, and safely log your daily feelings through Zen Mode.

How MindMappR works

A simple three-step process to better mental awareness.

1

Answer simple questions
